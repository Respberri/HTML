About The Members

I was born at Balanga, Bataan in ICMC. At an early age, my family and I transferred to Manila to live there. Over the next couple of years we lived Caloocan City.

Before I went to 6th grade, my family transferred back to Balanga, Bataan and I continued my studies there until today. I enjoy doing illustrations and playing games in my free time. I also like eating. Sometimes, I enjoy endulging in music via playing the piano.

After graduating in SHS, I decided to transfer to BPSU as it was relatively close to our home. This decision helped my family and I save up. My mother is a single mom and she has been working overseas to provide for me and my siblings.

During this time, I enrolled to BPSU. Over the course of a couple semesters, my mother decided to retire temporarily before having birth to my youngest sibling.

In my time at BPSU, I enjoyed learning and getting to know new people. I also further endugled on my hobbies and aspire to create a living off of them some day.
